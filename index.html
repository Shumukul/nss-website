Welcome to NSS Unit

National Service Scheme (NSS) ek youth development program hai jo Bharat Sarkar ke Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports ke under chalaya jata hai. Iska main objective students ko samaj seva aur community development ke liye inspire karna hai.

About NSS

NSS History:

National Service Scheme ki shuruaat 24 September 1969 ko Mahatma Gandhi ke birth centenary year par hui thi. Iska aim students ko samaj seva ke prati jagruk banana hai.
NSS Benefits:

• Personality Development
• Leadership Skill Development
• Social Awareness
• Community Service Experience
• Government Certificate
• Career Opportunities me advantage
